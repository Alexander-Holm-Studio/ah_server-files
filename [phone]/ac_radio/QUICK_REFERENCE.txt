┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ HOW RADIO WORKS                                                  │
├──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  1. Player needs "radio" item in inventory                       │
│  2. Use the item to open radio interface                         │
│  3. Type a frequency and click "Connect"                         │
│  4. Press & hold Z to talk on that frequency                     │
│  5. All players on same frequency will hear you                  │
│  6. Radio has realistic effect (sounds like real radio)          │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
